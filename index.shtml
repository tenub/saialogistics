The goal of logistics should not simply be to keep the supply chain moving, but to proactively manage it so that every action, every decision, yields as much productivity and savings as possible. That is the benefit and power of The RL Services Group.

The RL Services Group, a Saia owned company, is a third party logistics team that operates independently. Leveraging the combined experience and expertise of our team members, we give our clients the added capabilities and insight to optimize their supply chain operations.

We possess a wide range of capabilities used to create customized solutions for our clients. For some, that involves working on-site to fill gaps in their logistics management. For others, it means working side-by-side as consultants to develop valuable tools and processes that control and contain costs and provide useful information.
